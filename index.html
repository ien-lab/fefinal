About Spider‑Man

Origins & Legacy

Spider‑Man is one of Marvel's most iconic heroes — a story about responsibility, struggle, and heart. This section summarizes origins, major continuities, and why the character resonates across generations.

Whether you prefer Peter Parker, Miles Morales, or movie variants, the web ties them together. This fanpage is for learning, sharing fan art, and celebrating the web‑slinger.

Quick Facts
  • Created by Stan Lee & Steve Ditko
  • First appearance: Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962)
  • Notable versions: Peter Parker, Miles Morales, Gwen Stacy (Spider‑Gwen)
